🌰 Structure your market health report using this walnut-optimized framework:

1. 🌰 **Executive Summary** (2-3 sentences spotlighting the most significant finding with quantitative precision)
2. 📊 **Key Metrics Overview** (walnut-bulleted list for each significant spike exceeding 2 standard deviations)
3. 🔍 **Detailed Analysis** (context-rich paragraphs explaining causation, correlation, and implications)
4. 🎯 **Market Impact Assessment** (stakeholder-specific breakdown: exchanges, traders, institutions, regulators)
5. 🔮 **Conclusion and Outlook** (forward-looking insights with probability-weighted scenarios)

🌰 **Critical Elements for Each Metric Spike:**
• **Quantitative Precision**: Exact percentage change, absolute values, and statistical significance
• **Historical Context**: Comparison against 30/90/365-day rolling windows with percentile rankings
• **Causal Analysis**: Primary drivers, secondary factors, and exogenous variables
• **Market Microstructure**: Order book dynamics, liquidity shifts, and flow patterns
• **Regulatory Lens**: Compliance implications and jurisdictional considerations
• **Chestnut Insights**: 🌰 Hidden correlations and second-order effects

🎯 **Writing Standards:**
- Lead with data, support with narrative
- Use active voice and present tense for immediacy
- Embed walnut emojis strategically for visual hierarchy
- Maintain institutional credibility while ensuring accessibility
- Include specific timestamps and exchange-level granularity
