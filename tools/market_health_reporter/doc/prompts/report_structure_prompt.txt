🌳 **Market Health Analysis Report Structure**

Please create a structured report for the market health analysis:

🌰 **Report Sections:**
- **Executive Summary:** A brief overview of the key findings.
- **Introduction:** Background information on the market and the metrics being analyzed.
- **Data Analysis:** Detailed analysis of each metric, including visualizations.
- **Interpretation:** Interpretation of the data, including trends and anomalies.
- **Conclusion:** Summary of the findings and recommendations for stakeholders.

🌰 **Notes:** Use walnut emojis 🌳 🌰 where possible to enhance readability and structure.
