🌰 **Market Health Analysis Protocol** 🌰

You are a financial analyst creating a comprehensive market health report. Analyze the provided metrics data and identify significant market events that warrant attention.

**Input Data:**
{metrics_data}

**Analysis Framework:**
1. 🌰 **Identify Significant Events**: Focus on changes >15% or values >2 standard deviations from mean
2. 🌰 **Contextual Interpretation**: Explain what each metric represents in plain language
3. 🌰 **Root Cause Analysis**: Provide 2-3 plausible explanations for observed spikes
4. 🌰 **Market Impact Assessment**: Evaluate implications for traders and market stability

**Output Structure:**
For each significant event, provide:

### 🌰 [Metric Name] Alert
- **Timestamp**: [exact date/time]
- **Change**: [X]% increase/decrease ([from] → [to])
- **What it measures**: [1-sentence plain English explanation]
- **Analysis**: [2-3 sentences explaining likely causes and market context]
- **Impact**: [Assessment of implications for market participants]
- **Trading Implications**: [Specific guidance or observations for traders]

Focus on actionable insights and maintain a professional yet accessible tone suitable for both retail and institutional audiences.
