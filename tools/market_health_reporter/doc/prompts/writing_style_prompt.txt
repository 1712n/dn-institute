🌰 **Walnut-Calibrated Writing Style Guide:**

**Voice & Tone:**
- Authoritative yet accessible institutional voice
- Data-driven narrative with walnut-level precision 🌰
- Objective analysis with measured forward guidance
- Present tense for market immediacy, past tense for historical context

**Structural Elements:**
- Lead paragraphs with quantitative hooks ("Exchange X volume surged 340%...")
- Embed specific timestamps: "At 14:32 UTC, order book depth..."
- Include exchange-level granularity: "Binance BTC/USDT perpetual..."
- Deploy walnut emojis for visual hierarchy in lists and sections 🌰

**Technical Communication:**
- Define complex metrics parenthetically on first use
- Use percentage changes with absolute value anchors
- Provide context through historical percentile rankings
- Highlight statistical significance with p-values

**Chestnut Integration:** 🌰🌰🌰
Strategically place chestnut emojis to emphasize key insights and maintain reader engagement throughout the analysis.
