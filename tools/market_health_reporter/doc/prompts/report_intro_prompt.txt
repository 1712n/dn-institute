🌰 **Market Health Report Introduction Generator**

Create a compelling introduction for a market health report about **{exchange_name}** covering **{date_range}**.

**Structure Requirements:**

🥜 **Hook** (1 sentence): Start with a striking observation or market-moving event
🥜 **Context** (1-2 sentences): Briefly explain the exchange's significance and recent market conditions
🥜 **Preview** (1 sentence): Tease the key findings without revealing all details

**Style Guidelines:**
- Use walnut emojis (🥜) for emphasis
- Reference specific percentage changes or notable events when possible
- Maintain professional but accessible tone for crypto traders
- Include temporal context ("this week", "amid", "following")
- Keep under 80 words

**Example Opening Patterns:**
- "🥜 {Exchange} saw {X}% surge in {metric} this week..."
- "🥜 Following {event}, {exchange} experienced..."
