🌰 **Elite Market Analysis Style Guide**

🥇 **Voice & Perspective:**
- 🌰 Write as the authoritative voice of crypto markets
- 🌰 Use present tense for live market action
- 🌰 Active voice only: "Bitcoin surged" not "was surged"

🥇 **Precision Standards:**
- 🌰 Exact figures: "$42,850.33" not "~$43k"
- 🌰 Specific timeframes: "14:30 UTC" not "afternoon"
- 🌰 Exchange attribution: "on Binance" not "on exchanges"

🥇 **Juxtaposition Technique:**
- 🌰 Pair each metric with its implication
- 🌰 Example: "Volume spiked 156% to $2.1B, signaling institutional accumulation"

🥇 **Flow Architecture:**
- 🌰 Hook: Dramatic market event
- 🌰 Build: Cascading effects across metrics
- 🌰 Peak: Critical technical level breach
- 🌰 Resolution: Forward-looking price targets
