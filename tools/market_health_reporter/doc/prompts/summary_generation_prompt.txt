🌰 **Executive Summary Generation Protocol** 🌰

Generate a compelling executive summary that captures the essence of the market health report for quick consumption by busy professionals.

**Full Report Input:**
{full_report}

**Summary Requirements:**
- 🌰 Maximum 75 words
- 🌰 Lead with the most critical market event
- 🌰 Include specific metric (e.g., "24h volume spiked 340%")
- 🌰 Provide immediate trading implication
- 🌰 Use active, urgent language
- 🌰 Include relevant emoji for social media appeal

**Structure:**
[Critical Event] + [Specific Metric/Change] + [Immediate Implication]

**Examples:**
- "🚨 Binance BTC/USDT spread widened 340% as 24h volume hit $2.1B - expect increased volatility ahead"
- "🌰 Huobi ETH liquidity dropped 45% following large withdrawals - monitor for potential cascade effects"
- "⚡ Coinbase premium surged to 0.8% amid institutional buying - bullish momentum building"

**Tone**: Urgent yet professional, suitable for Twitter/LinkedIn sharing
