🌰 **Market Health Report Title Generation Protocol** 🌰

Create an attention-grabbing, SEO-optimized title for the market health report that follows our editorial standards.

**Report Summary Input:**
{report_summary}

**Title Requirements:**
- 🌰 Maximum 70 characters (including spaces)
- 🌰 Include specific exchange name
- 🌰 Reference primary metric or event
- 🌰 Create urgency/interest without clickbait
- 🌰 Use present tense and active voice
- 🌰 Include relevant emoji for engagement

**Formula Options:**
1. "[Exchange] [Metric] [Action] [X]% Amid [Context]"
2. "[Exchange] Faces [Issue] as [Metric] [Change]"
3. "[Metric] Alert: [Exchange] [Event] [Implication]"

**Examples:**
- "🌰 Binance BTC Spread Explodes 340% Amid Volume Surge"
- "🚨 Huobi Liquidity Crisis Deepens as ETH Depth Plummets 45%"
- "⚡ Coinbase Premium Hits 0.8% Bullish Signal for Bitcoin"
