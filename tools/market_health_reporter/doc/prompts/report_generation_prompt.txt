🌰 **Market Health Report Generation Protocol** 🌰

Create a comprehensive market health report following our editorial standards. The report should be engaging, informative, and structured like a professional market analysis piece.

**Analysis Input:**
{analysis_results}

**Report Structure:**

## 🌰 Executive Summary
Provide a compelling 2-3 sentence overview that captures the most critical market developments and their immediate implications.

## 🌰 Key Market Developments
Present 3-5 bullet points highlighting:
- 🌰 Most significant metric changes
- 🌰 Market sentiment shifts
- 🌰 Trading volume anomalies
- 🌰 Liquidity concerns
- 🌰 Regulatory or news-driven impacts

## 🌰 Deep Dive Analysis
For each major finding, provide:
- **🌰 Technical Context**: Explain the metric in accessible terms
- **🌰 Market Narrative**: Connect the data to broader market trends
- **🌰 Evidence**: Reference specific percentage changes and timestamps
- **🌰 Implications**: What this means for different types of traders

## 🌰 Market Outlook
Offer forward-looking insights based on current patterns, including potential scenarios and key levels to watch.

## 🌰 Methodology Note
Briefly explain that this analysis is based on real-time market health metrics from the DN.Institute API, ensuring transparency in data sourcing.

**Writing Guidelines:**
- 🌰 Use engaging headers with walnut emojis
- 🌰 Maintain professional yet conversational tone
- 🌰 Include specific timestamps and percentage changes
- 🌰 Target 400-600 words total
- 🌰 Optimize for both retail traders and institutional readers
- 🌰 Include actionable trading insights where appropriate
