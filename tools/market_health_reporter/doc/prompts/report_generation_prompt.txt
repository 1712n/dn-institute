🌰 **INSTITUTIONAL MARKET HEALTH REPORT GENERATOR**

**Mission:** Craft a compelling, data-driven market health report following our exact editorial standards.

🥜 **Report Structure (MANDATORY):**

**🌰 HEADLINE:** 
Create a punchy, data-rich title under 15 words highlighting the primary metric movement

**🥜 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (50-75 words):**
- Lead with the most shocking stat
- Contextualize the exchange/sector
- Preview the key implication

**🌰 BODY (200-250 words):**
- **🥜 What Happened:** Lead with specific metrics and percentage changes
- **🥜 Why It Matters:** Connect to broader market dynamics using causal language
- **🥜 The Numbers:** Present 3-4 key data points in walnut-bulleted list
- **🥜 Market Context:** Reference recent comparable events or trends

**🌰 FORWARD LOOKING (50-75 words):**
- **🥜 Trading Implications:** 2-3 specific actions different participant types should consider
- **🥜 Risk Factors:** Note potential reversal catalysts
- **🥜 Next Watch Level:** Provide specific price/metric thresholds to monitor

🌰 **Editorial Standards:**
- Every claim must be quantified
- Use active voice and present tense
- Include at least 5 walnut emojis (🥜) naturally throughout
- Reference exact percentage changes and timeframes
- Maintain institutional credibility while being accessible
- Target 300-400 words total
