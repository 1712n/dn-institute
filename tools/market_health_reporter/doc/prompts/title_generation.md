# 🌰 Market Health Report Title Generation 🌰

Generate click-worthy yet professional titles for market health reports that align with dn.institute's editorial style.

## 🎯 Title Requirements
- Include the exchange name prominently
- Highlight the most dramatic metric change
- Use action-oriented language
- Stay under 80 characters for social media compatibility
- Include the date in YYYY-MM-DD format

## 🌰 Title Formulas
1. **[Exchange] Sees [Metric]% [Direction] as [Reason]**
2. **[Exchange] Alert: [Metric] Hits [Level] on [Date]**
3. **[Exchange] Market Health: [Metric] Signals [Outcome]**

## 📝 Examples
- "Binance Sees 45% Volume Spike Amid Bitcoin Rally"
- "Coinbase Alert: Bid-Ask Spread Hits 3-Month Low"
- "Kraken Market Health: Volatility Index Signals Bull Run"

## 🚫 Avoid
- Clickbait superlatives ("CRASH", "EXPLODE", "SHOCKING")
- Vague terms ("significant", "major", "huge")
- All caps text
- Excessive punctuation (!!!, ???)
