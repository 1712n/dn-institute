## Analysis Procedure

### Step 1: Compute Derived Values

Before writing, compute these for each hourly data point:
- **K-S critical value** = 1.36 / sqrt(`tradecount`)
- **K-S verdict**: "REJECT" if `benfordlawtest` > critical value, else "PASS"
- **Buy/sell deviation**: distance of `buysellratio` from the 0.4–0.6 normal range
- **VV-correlation status**: "SUSPICIOUS" if `vvcorrelation` < 0.4 for 3+ consecutive hours

### Step 2: Identify Anomalous Windows

Scan the time series for windows where TWO OR MORE metrics deviate simultaneously. Rank these windows by severity (number of co-occurring anomalies). Only report windows where at least two of the following hold:

| Signal | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|
| K-S test rejects Benford's Law | `benfordlawtest` > 1.36 / sqrt(`tradecount`) |
| Volume-Volatility decoupling | `vvcorrelation` < 0.4 for 3+ consecutive hours |
| Buy/Sell ratio outside normal | `buysellratio` < 0.4 or > 0.6 |
| Volume distribution anomaly | `volumedist` deviates from power-law shape |
| Time-of-trade clustering | `timeoftrade` shows uniform or single-spike pattern |
| VWAP divergence | Persistent gap between `vwap` and market price |

### Step 3: Cross-Correlation Patterns

When reporting anomalies, explicitly check for these known manipulation signatures:

1. **Wash trading**: Volume distribution breaks power law + K-S rejects Benford's + low VV-correlation (volume without volatility impact)
2. **Spoofing/layering**: VWAP diverges from price + abnormal buy/sell ratio (one-sided pressure that doesn't move price proportionally)
3. **Bot activity**: Time-of-trade shows periodic spikes or uniform distribution + steady buy/sell ratio during volatile periods

### Step 4: Determine Verdict

- **Strong evidence**: 3+ metrics deviate simultaneously in the same time window
- **Moderate evidence**: 2 metrics deviate simultaneously
- **Insufficient evidence**: Only isolated single-metric deviations — report this honestly

## Metric Reference

| Metric | Key | Normal Range | Anomaly Signal |
|--------|-----|-------------|----------------|
| Volume-Volatility Correlation | `vvcorrelation` | > 0.4 | Consistently < 0.4 |
| First-Digit Distribution | `firstdigitdist` | Conforms to Benford's Law | Disproportionate higher digits |

| Volume Distribution | `volumedist` | Power-law (right-skewed) | Symmetric or spike-heavy |
