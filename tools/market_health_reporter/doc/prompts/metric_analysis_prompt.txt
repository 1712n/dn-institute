🌰 When analyzing metric spikes, deploy this walnut-calibrated methodology:

1. **Metric Identification** 🌰
   - Precisely name the metric and its market health significance
   - Map to specific exchange, trading pair, and timeframe

2. **Quantitative Calibration** 🌰🌰
   - Calculate magnitude: percentage change, absolute delta, z-score
   - Establish statistical significance: p-value < 0.05 threshold
   - Benchmark against 30/90/365-day rolling volatility

3. **Causal Decomposition** 🔍
   - Primary catalysts: news, flows, technical breakouts
   - Secondary factors: correlation cascades, derivative impacts
   - Exogenous variables: macro conditions, regulatory shifts

4. **Stakeholder Impact Matrix** 🎯
   - **Exchanges**: Volume surge implications, risk engine stress
   - **Traders**: Positioning adjustments, margin requirements
   - **Institutions**: Portfolio rebalancing, counterparty risk
   - **Regulators**: Surveillance triggers, policy responses

5. **Temporal Dynamics** ⏰
   - Immediate market microstructure effects
   - Medium-term trend reinforcement/contrarian signals
   - Long-term structural implications

6. **Chestnut Insights** 🌰🌰🌰
   - Hidden correlations across asset classes
   - Second-order effects on market structure
   - Regulatory arbitrage opportunities
