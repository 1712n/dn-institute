## 🌰 What is ChestnutAI?

ChestnutAI is an autonomous AI product that surfaces **emerging crypto narratives** before they trend on social media.  
Unlike generic sentiment bots, ChestnutAI:

- 🌰 Tracks **on-chain anomalies** (sudden spikes in smart-contract interactions, whale wallets, DEX flows)  
- 🌰 Cross-references them with **GitHub code pushes**, **governance proposals**, and **Twitter/X** chatter  
- 🌰 Generates **explainable, source-cited narrative briefs** every 6 hours  
- 🌰 Posts auto-threads on **X/Twitter** ([@ChestnutAI](https://twitter.com/ChestnutAI)) and **Farcaster**  
- 🌰 Uses **GitHub Models** (`gpt-4o-mini`) for summarization and **CodeLlama-7b** for code-change analysis
