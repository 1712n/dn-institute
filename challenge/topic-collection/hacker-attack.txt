Gate.io confirmed a 51% attack on Ethereum Classic in January 2019. 54,200 ETC worth of transactions were reversed. The attacker's account had been registered 3 months earlier and passed KYC checks.
South Korean intelligence confirmed that North Korean hackers have stolen over $1.2 billion in crypto since 2017. Lazarus Group attacks on exchanges are funding North Korea's weapons programs.
Chainalysis reports that $2.2 billion was stolen in crypto hacks in 2024, with DPRK-affiliated hackers responsible for $1.3 billion of that. North Korea's share of total crypto hacking keeps growing.
Wormhole bridge was exploited for $320 million in February 2022. The attacker minted 120,000 wETH without proper collateral. Jump Trading covered the losses. Bridge exploits became the new exchange hacks.
Nomad bridge was drained for $190 million in August 2022 in a chaotic free-for-all exploit. Hundreds of users joined in once the vulnerability was publicly visible. It was described as "the first decentralized hack."
