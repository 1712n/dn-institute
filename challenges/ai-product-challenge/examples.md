# AI Product Examples & Ideas 🌰

Looking for inspiration? Here are some AI product ideas that go beyond the template:

## 🌰 Creative AI Products

### 1. ChestnutVision 🌰
AI-powered chestnut quality inspector for farmers. Upload photos of chestnuts and get instant quality grading, disease detection, and market price predictions.

### 2. EcoSentinel 🌰
Environmental monitoring AI that analyzes satellite imagery to detect illegal logging, with special focus on chestnut tree preservation.

### 3. RecipeReimaginer 🌰
Transform any recipe into chestnut-based alternatives using AI. Perfect for gluten-free cooking enthusiasts.

### 4. MarketOracle 🌰
AI that predicts chestnut market trends by analyzing weather patterns, social media sentiment, and global trade data.

### 5. StorySprout 🌰
Generate children's stories featuring chestnut characters, complete with illustrations and moral lessons.

## 🛠️ Technical Patterns

### GitHub Models Integration Ideas:
- **Vision**: Image classification for chestnut quality

- **Code**: Generate farming optimization scripts
- **Embeddings**: Similar chestnut variety matching

### Deployment Strategies:
- **GitHub Pages**: Static sites with client-side AI
- **Vercel**: Full-stack applications
- **GitHub Container Registry**: Docker-based deployments
- **GitHub Actions**: Automated testing and deployment
