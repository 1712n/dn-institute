    Detection Methodology

    Benford's Law Analysis

    Statistical test comparing first-digit distribution of trade values against expected natural distribution. High deviation indicates potential data fabrication.

    Buy/Sell Ratio Analysis

    Monitors extreme imbalances in buy vs sell volumes that indicate coordinated manipulation or spoofing activity.

    Volume Distribution

    Identifies abnormal clustering in trade sizes that suggests wash trading through round-number detection and repetitive pattern analysis.

    Time-of-Trade Distribution

    Detects automated trading patterns by analyzing temporal clustering of trades at specific millisecond intervals.

    VWAP Deviation

    Volume-Weighted Average Price analysis identifies artificial price movements disconnected from genuine market activity.
