Welcome to TrendLens AI, your window into the ever-evolving world of technology! 🌍
Here, our AI bot processes GitHub project data to bring you actionable insights and reports on emerging trends.
Stay ahead of the curve with our automatically generated intelligence briefs. 💡

To request a new report, simply open a GitHub Issue in our repository and comment `/trendlens <your_topic>`.
For example: `/trendlens serverless architectures` or `/trendlens quantum computing frameworks`. 🌰

All reports are initially marked as `draft` and require a maintainer review before becoming public. This ensures quality and accuracy of the AI-generated content.
