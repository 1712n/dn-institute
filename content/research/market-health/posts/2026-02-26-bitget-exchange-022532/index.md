## Background on Wash Trading
Wash trading is a form of market manipulation where an individual or entity buys and sells a security, such as a cryptocurrency, with the intention of deceiving others about the security's trading activity. This can be done to create the illusion of high demand, attract more investors, and ultimately drive up the price. Wash trading is prohibited in most jurisdictions, including the United States, where it is considered a form of securities fraud.
