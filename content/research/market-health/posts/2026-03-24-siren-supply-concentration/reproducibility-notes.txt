SIREN case study reproducibility notes
======================================

This note maps the SIREN case study artifacts to the market-health bounty's
data-backed submission criteria.

Artifact map
------------

- index.md: article text, citations, methodology, event table, monitoring rules,
  and conclusion.
- siren-event-signals.csv: source-linked event timeline for public price,
  concentration, and drawdown claims.
- siren-coingecko-market-window.csv: CoinGecko market-chart observations for the
  March and April event windows.
- siren-window-summary.csv: generated high, low, drawdown, and volume summary
  statistics.
- siren-event-window-tests.csv: baseline-versus-event z-score checks for log
  hourly volume and absolute period returns.
- siren-price-volume-window.png: chart generated from the committed market
  window data.

Review checklist
----------------

1. Confirm the article does not treat wallet-cluster estimates as proven intent.
2. Confirm every public narrative claim has a source URL in the article or event
   signal table.
3. Confirm the drawdown and z-score values in index.md match the committed CSV
   summaries.
4. Confirm the chart uses the committed CoinGecko market-window data and does
   not depend on private API keys or uncommitted data.
