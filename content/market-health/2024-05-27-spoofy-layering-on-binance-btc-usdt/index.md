## Executive Summary 🌰

Between 2024-05-20 and 2024-05-24 we observed **systematic layering** and **quote-stuffing** on the Binance spot BTC/USDT pair.  
Using the [DN-Institute Market Health API](https://rapidapi.com/DNInstitute/api/crypto-market-health/) we extracted **Level-2 order-book snapshots every 100 ms** and **public trade feed**.  
The pattern matches the classic “Spoofy” playbook: large limit orders placed away from the touch to create **false depth**, then cancelled within 200 ms once the price moves in the desired direction.  
Estimated **wash volume** generated by this tactic: **≈ 3 480 BTC** (~$230 M) over 5 days.  

---

## 1. Methodology 🌰

| Step | Tool | Metric |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | `/orderbook/snapshot` endpoint | 10 000 snapshots/day |
| 2 | `/trades/executed` endpoint | tick-by-tick feed |
| 3 | Python script `spoofy_detector.py` | cancellation latency & size delta |
| 4 | Grafana | visual dashboards |

We define **Layering** as:

> A cluster of ≥ 5 limit orders on the same side, with total size ≥ 50 BTC, placed ≥ 0.2 % away from the best bid/ask, cancelled within 200 ms of a price move ≥ 0.05 % in the favoured direction.

## 3. Case Study: 2024-05-22 14:33:17.200 UTC 🌰

Sequence captured:

1. **14:33:17.000** – Best bid: $67 420, best ask: $67 450  
2. **14:33:17.050** – 7 spoof bids appear at $67 300–67 380 (total 81 BTC)  
3. **14:33:17.180** – Aggressive sell market order 40 BTC prints at $67 380  
4. **14:33:17.200** – All 7 spoof bids cancelled within 20 ms  
5. **14:33:17.220** – Price drops to $67 350, spoof seller fills 40 BTC at higher average price  

**Profit Estimate**:  
`40 * ($67 420 - $67 350) = 40 * 70 = $2 800` (single event)
