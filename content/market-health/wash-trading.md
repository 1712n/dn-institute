## Introduction 🌰
Market manipulation is a significant concern in the cryptocurrency space, and wash trading is one of the most common forms of manipulation. In this article, we will explore instances of wash trading on Huobi, a popular cryptocurrency exchange, and provide insights into the methods used to manipulate the market.

## Methods of Market Manipulation 🌰
Wash trading is a form of market manipulation where an individual or group creates the illusion of trading activity by buying and selling the same asset repeatedly. This can be done to manipulate the price of the asset, create the illusion of market demand, or to launder money.
## Data Analysis 🌰
To analyze the instances of wash trading on Huobi, we used data from the Crypto Market Health API, which provides basic metrics related to wash trading activities. The data showed that Huobi had a significant amount of wash trading activity, particularly in the TRX and DOGE markets.
### Market Venue Orderbook Snapshots 🌰
We also analyzed market venue orderbook snapshots to gain a deeper understanding of the wash trading activities. The snapshots showed that there were a large number of orders being placed and canceled in a short period, which is a common indicator of wash trading.

## Conclusion 🌰
In conclusion, our analysis showed that Huobi had a significant amount of wash trading activity, particularly in the TRX and DOGE markets.
